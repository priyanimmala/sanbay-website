Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about managed IT services and support contracts.

What's included in an Annual Maintenance Contract?

AMC coverage typically includes scheduled preventive maintenance, priority technical support, and hardware/software issue resolution — exact scope is defined per contract based on your infrastructure.

How does Managed Print Services reduce our costs?

MPS consolidates supply procurement, optimizes fleet usage, and proactively maintains printers to reduce both cost-per-page and IT support tickets related to printing.

Can Sanbay manage IT for multiple office locations?

Yes, our PAN India field services network supports organizations with multiple offices, providing consistent service levels across locations.

What's the difference between Managed IT Services and Field Services?

Managed IT Services covers ongoing monitoring and remote support; Field Services covers on-site technical work — installation, hardware support, and in-person troubleshooting when remote support isn't enough.

How quickly can your field team respond to an on-site issue?

Response times depend on your service agreement tier and location relative to our PAN India offices — this is defined clearly in your AMC or support contract.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about enterprise network design and deployment.

How long does an enterprise network deployment typically take?

Timelines depend on site count and complexity, but a single-site deployment typically takes 2-6 weeks from design to go-live, including site survey, configuration and testing.

What is SD-WAN and why would my organization need it?

SD-WAN intelligently manages traffic across multiple network connections, reducing dependency on expensive dedicated lines while improving reliability — ideal if you operate across multiple branches or offices.

Can you upgrade our wireless network without disrupting daily operations?

Yes, we plan phased rollouts and schedule installation work during low-traffic periods to minimize disruption to your operations.

Do you provide network security alongside connectivity?

Network security is part of our Firewall & Security Solutions offering, and we frequently design network and security architecture together for a unified, secure deployment.

How do you ensure wireless coverage in large facilities like hospitals or factories?

We conduct a detailed site survey and RF planning before deployment, mapping coverage requirements against building materials, interference sources and density needs to eliminate dead zones.

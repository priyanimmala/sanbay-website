Who We Are

Sanbay Networks Pvt. Ltd. is a 20+ year enterprise technology company serving large organizations across Healthcare, Government, BFSI, Manufacturing, Education, and IT & ITES sectors.

With partnerships including HP, HPE, VMware, Microsoft, AWS, Nutanix, and RedHat — Sanbay is not a typical IT reseller. We are a trusted infrastructure partner for India's most demanding organizations.

Our work spans data centers and server rooms, enterprise office environments, IT infrastructure and networking equipment — always enterprise-grade, always future-ready.
